Legislative Rules
June 1st Exemption
If a pension account is commenced on or after June 1st in any financial year, the mandatory minimum drawdown requirement for that pension account is $0 for the remainder of that financial year.
Bring-Forward rules
Enables making non-concessional contributions (NCC) of up to 3 times the annual cap in a single financial year, depending on your Total Super Balance (TSB) on the prior 30 June.
Notice of Intent (NOI)
A form submitted to your fund to claim a tax deduction for personal contributions. The claimed portion is taxed at 15% inside the fund, converting it to Concessional Contribution (CC).
